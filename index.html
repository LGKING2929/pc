Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Lakshya PC build

How long will this build stay relevant?

+

This build is designed to remain competitive for 5+ years. The AM5 platform will support future CPU generations through 2027, and the RTX 4070 Ti Super has enough VRAM and performance for upcoming games. The modular design allows for easy upgrades when needed.

What's the difference between the three tiers?

+

The main differences are in the CPU and GPU. Top tier features the RTX 4070 Ti Super for 4K gaming, while mid and budget tiers use the RTX 4070. The budget tier also uses the 7700X CPU instead of the 7800X3D, offering great value while maintaining excellent performance.

Can I upgrade individual components later?

+

Absolutely! The build is designed for easy upgrades. You can add more RAM, additional storage, upgrade the GPU, or even swap the CPU. The 850W PSU provides headroom for more powerful components, and the case has excellent accessibility.

How quiet is this build during operation?

+

Very quiet! The AIO cooler and optimized fan curves keep noise under 35dB during normal use. The Lian Li fans are designed for low noise, and the case provides excellent sound dampening. You'll barely notice it running.

What kind of performance can I expect?

+

Expect 4K gaming at 60+ FPS in most titles with the top tier, 1440p high refresh gaming across all tiers, and excellent content creation performance. The system handles streaming, video editing, and multitasking with ease.
